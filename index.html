❓ How the Greenhouse Works

🌿 Discoveries — the whole point

Every brew rolls one of these outcomes:

  • Named mutation — your seeds + conditions match a recipe in the Book. Biggest payouts, rarity-weighted.
  • Wild [Crop] — no curated recipe matched. Earns a common discovery + coins (book entry only — not plantable).
  • Seedling Burst — fallback when a hybrid attempt fails on a low tier.
  • Failure — instability rolled too high. Just XP consolation.

Named discoveries go into the Book AND become plantable on your farm — flip the seed strip toggle. Wild discoveries are Book-only.

Pick two seeds. Same seed = pure splice. Different seeds = cross-breed (Tier 4 only).

Set conditions. Each slot (Water / Light / Soil / Catalyst) has options that raise the rarity score and instability.

Pay + brew. Cost = seed splice fee + slot costs. Brew time depends on your tier (60m → 30m).

Claim when ready. The brew rolls a possible mutation per the section above.

Upgrade tiers for more slots, faster brews, and at Tier 4: cross-breeding two different seeds.

You're at Lv 20. The new Reset Farm button on the toolbar (between Greenhouse and Horse) lets you wipe the run and start fresh — but you keep a permanent bonus.

How to Play

A quick reference for the basics. Hover anything in-game for live tooltips.

🌱 The Loop

  1. Plow a grass tile inside the fence.
  2. Pick a seed at the bottom, then Plant on plowed soil.
  3. Water a tile (2¢) to grow it 50% faster.
  4. Harvest when the tile glows yellow for coins + XP.

🛒 Shop & Place

  1. Open Shop → Trees / Decor / Animals / Buildings, click BUY.
  2. A ghost preview follows your cursor — click a free tile to place.
  3. Use Sell on a placed item for a 50% refund.
  4. Move tool picks up + drops buildings to relocate them.

🦏 Animals & Staff

  1. Place a Big Red Barn to open the animal pen.
  2. Click an animal to Collect, Slaughter, or Pet.
  3. At higher levels, Staff Hall hires farmhands to automate the busywork.

📱 Camera & Tools

  1. Drag with a tool to paint across tiles.
  2. Right-drag or Shift+drag to pan the camera.
  3. Mouse wheel zooms in/out.
